about Spherical Communications

Spherical Communications is a boutique digital marketing agency based in New York City, specializing in travel, hospitality and lifestyle brands. The internet has changed the dynamic of connecting a product with a customer. It is no longer about buying attention and pushing a product with paid advertising. Building a brand and making a sale is about connecting a story (product) to a community (customer). With a foundation in social media, we develop and execute digital strategies that connect your company to the right community through innovative content and communication.

Story (Product)

Products are discovered and purchased because of the story as much as the physical specifications of the product. The story that the customer tells themselves. The story that the customer tells a friend.

Through interactive media, we help you create and tell the story of your brand online.

Community (Customer)

Word of mouth is a primary driver of product discovery. Brands now have the opportunity to be a part of the conversation about their product through social media.

We help you establish the voice of your brand in digital communication and connect your brand to a community of customers.
